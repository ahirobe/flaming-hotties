How our Joke Generator Works:

                

                

                    

                        
                            
                            
                        
                        
The Prompt

                        
The Joke Generator takes in user input to use as the basis of the joke.

                    

                    

                        
                            
                            
                        
                        
The Model

                        
There are two models. Each model is trained to generate a certain type of joke based on the type of model it is.

                    

                    

                        
                            
                            
                        
                        
The Result

                        
The AI will analyze the prompt and come up with a joke befitting the prompt.

Kaggle

                                    
Datasets/Pre-processing.

                                    
                                    
For our generator, we collected data by using a couple of datasets from Kaggle. Specifically, the ones we chose were the Reddit Dad Jokes and the Walks in a Bar datasets. In total, there were 1,789 jokes from both of the datasets mentioned, but we also had to filter the ones that were nonsensical, had profanity, and that were just inappropriate in general.


                                    
                                

                            

                        

                    

                

            

        

        
        

            

                

                    

                    

                        

                            

                                

                                    
                                    
GPT-Neo

                                    
Model

                                    
                                    
GPT-Neo is a pre-trained language model released by EleutherAI. Although not as good as GPT-3, it offers better performance compared to GPT-2 while also being free to use.
